Direct matrix multiplication without parity will give the square root of the ℝ4 metric, with 𝜎𝜇2=𝟙, 𝜎𝜇−1=𝜎𝜇

This makes it possible for the spacetime momentum spin representation to be connected to the concept of classical fermions. Spinors belong to the light cone projection ℂℙ1. If 𝑝spin is on the light cone, then its square 12(𝑝spin ◊𝑝 spin +𝑝 spin 𝑝 spin◊)=𝑔(𝑝spin,𝑝spin)𝟙=0. This seems to be related to the Pauli exclusion principle.
